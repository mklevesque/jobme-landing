How does JobMe work for recruiters?

You can see JobMe as a three-step journey: set up your company space, publish & match, then pilot your hiring.

1. Create your company space

You create a dedicated space for your organisation: description, industry, location, size, values, employer brand. You can invite other recruiters or managers to join with different roles (HR admin, recruiter, viewer).

This space becomes your recruitment hub in Ivory Coast: all your offers, candidates, talent pools and stats are centralised there.

2. Publish your jobs & let the AI work

You create a job by entering the role, location, contract type, salary range, required skills, perks, languages, start date and context.

The AI can help you generate or rewrite the job description from a simple title or an existing document. Once published, JobMe starts suggesting matches to talents and prioritising applications for you.

3. Manage your pipeline & decisions

Each job has a visual pipeline: new candidates, our selection for you, to review, shortlist, interview, hired, rejected. You can annotate, favourite, and move candidates from one stage to another.
